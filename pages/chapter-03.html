Tikaro was Dried Fingered and running through Crucifixion Woods when Oden the Armless made his appearance. Early on in the run, the Puppeteer sent poem #10, from which we have gathered that there were not four puppets, as originally though, but five. The last, “eastward” one is the Heart of Spears, which at this time has not appeared yet.

Narcolept, who will be one of the main people getting the Puppeteer on camera, recorded a couple of fights with Oden during this event:

Just as during the previous event, Oden the Armless was eventually replaced by Lone Darkwraith when Tikaro opened the doors of the Cathedral of the Deep. The puppet was invading and sometimes grabbing mad white signs in the Cleansing Chapel. After summoning Narcolept for a fight, the Puppeteer sent him poem #11. This one is likely a continuation of #10 in foreshadowing the appearance of Heart of Spears.

Redflame Erik appeared in the Farron Keep, trailing the run as had become common in the last month. Also, around this point it could be vaguely seen that the puppets had their own designated areas. Redflame Erik was tied to the Farron Keep, White Vagrant to Irithyll, Lone Darkwraith to Cathedral of the Deep, and Oden the Armless to Irithyll Dungeon. They didn’t invade only these areas, but rather were tied to them thematically. This will be seen clearly during the final event, almost four months in the future.

At the end of the event, the Puppeteer joined the Discord server and sent poem #12 himself. Joining, posting a new poem, and leaving would become the main way he would communicate with us for a while.

Several days later, on January 25, the Puppeteer sent friend requests to the moderators of the SL90 Discord. Narcolept received poem #13, along with a string of broken AAAs. Alex got a lot more AAAs that are qualified here as #14. Tikaro and Raff both sent friend requests to the Puppeteer, and both got #15 and #16 (haha), respectively. The only poem out of the four things sent expanded upon the then cryptic foreshadowing of what would happen to Redflame Erik and Lone Darkwraith during the next event.
